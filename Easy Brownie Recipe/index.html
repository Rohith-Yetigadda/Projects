Easy Brownies

Ingredients

  • ½ cup (115g) unsalted butter
  • 1 cup (200g) granulated sugar
  • 2 large eggs
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • ⅓ cup (40g) unsweetened cocoa powder
  • ½ cup (65g) all-purpose flour
  • ¼ teaspoon salt
  • ¼ teaspoon baking powder

Instructions

  1. Melt ½ cup butter.
  2. Mix in 1 cup sugar, 2 eggs, 1 tsp vanilla.
  3. Stir in ⅓ cup cocoa, ½ cup flour, pinch salt, ¼ tsp baking powder.
  4. Pour into a greased 8x8 pan.
  5. Bake at 350°F (175°C) for ~20–25 min.
Cool and Enjoy Your Brownies!
